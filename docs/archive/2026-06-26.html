Good morning, Littleton! The most-watched story heading into the weekend remains the Shaker Lane School replacement project — the Building Committee met earlier this month and a community forum took place in May, so attention now turns to what comes next in the design and approval process for the 64-year-old elementary school. It's also been a celebratory stretch for local education, with Littleton Middle School earning a Blue Ribbon nomination and both the middle and high schools receiving state recognition for excellence. Rounding out a busy week, the Planning Board, School Committee, Conservation Commission, and Select Board all held meetings in recent days, keeping a full slate of town business moving forward.
